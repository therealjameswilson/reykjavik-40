# Reykjavik 40

**40th Anniversary of the Reykjavik Summit**
*October 11–12, 1986 → October 11–12, 2026*

This repository powers a public-facing website commemorating the 40th anniversary of the historic Reykjavik Summit between President Ronald Reagan and General Secretary Mikhail Gorbachev — one of the most consequential diplomatic meetings of the Cold War.

## About the Summit

The Reykjavik Summit took place at Höfði House in Reykjavik, Iceland, on October 11–12, 1986. Though it ended without a formal agreement, the talks between Reagan and Gorbachev laid the groundwork for the 1987 Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty — the first arms control agreement to eliminate an entire class of nuclear weapons.

## Project Purpose

This site is built to:
- Tell the story of the Reykjavik Summit through primary source documents
- Surface declassified records, diplomatic cables, and historical materials
- Provide accessible, searchable context for educators, researchers, and the public
- Mark the 40th anniversary with a digital public history resource
