    Hour-by-Hour Timeline

    Anchored to the Iceland Chronology in Foreign Relations of the United States, 1981–1988, Volume VI, Document 1 — a Memorandum for the Record dated 18 October 1986 that reconstructs the four days in Iceland. Documents released in Volumes V and VI are folded in as marker events; times of day are parsed from the chronology text. Photographs from the White House Photographic Collection (Ronald Reagan Presidential Library) are placed alongside the moments they depict on 11–12 October 1986.
